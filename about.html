About Us

,


What can we reveal about ourselves that won’t take away too much of the mysteriousness surrounding our identities?

Mysterio mysteries was created by two people who loved solving problems/mysteries. Eventually, we thought wouldn’t it be more fun to make our own mysteries and get people to solve them and thus Mysterio mysteries was created. We originally debated about format our game should take and we decided on this one as we wanted to make it as accessible as possible.

Out of our team of two, one of us is a bit over dramatic and likes to make up completely outrageous plots, the other one is there to help bring the mysteries back down to reality and works on the plausibility side of things.

We hope to be able to expand Mysterio mysteries in the future.
