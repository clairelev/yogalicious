Suspect 5: Chef

Age: 43
Occupation: Chef
Reason for attending: Has lost passion for their current job and is hoping the yoga retreat will help relax them and allow them the clarity to re-evaluate their priorities and where to go in life.

Statement

I didn’t know Drew very well, we mostly had different schedules so outside of group meal times we had very little interaction. They seemed pleasant enough but honestly I don’t know what they were doing here in the first place. Yogalicious is all about the health of mind and body and yet every meal time without fail they ordered a burger. And do you know what the worst part is? They would request to have all the salad removed. Who does that?

They clearly weren’t taking the retreat seriously at all, if you ask me I think someone saw that as a sign of blatant disrespect for everything we are all hoping to achieve here and was just fed up with it. Not that I think that’s a valid reason to kill someone over or anything, just I can see where they might have been coming from, you know?

Me? Oh I was in my room all afternoon. I decided to skip the yoga class that afternoon as I was feeling unwell and just needed some time to do some peaceful meditation by myself. I had a very productive session and didn’t leave my room until I was informed of the murder.


Detective's Observations

Definitely hiding something though no known confrontations with the victim. Unable to provide an alibi for the time of the murder. Was clearly paying a lot of attention to the victim to be aware of their food preferences at each meal.
