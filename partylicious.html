Data breach at Partylicious

Chad has decided he needs a change in life and has left Brad behind to join the ranks of Partylicious, a fabulous shop that meets all your party needs. Shortly after joining the company, there’s a huge data breach and poor Chad is getting the blame. The company is losing money and customers every second when the culprit is still at large.

It’s down to you as the detective and great friend of Chad’s to find out who the guilty person is behind the data breach is before it’s too late to be able to save Chad’s job, Partylicious’s customers and data.
